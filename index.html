About Us

At Paddarai surf lodge, we believe a getaway should feel like coming home. Surrounded by nature and just steps from the waves, our lodge offers a cozy and welcoming space where surfers and travelers can relax, recharge, and enjoy unforgettable surf adventures. Whether you're chasing the perfect swell or simply soaking in the peaceful surroundings, every moment here is meant for your comfort, joy, and connection with nature.

Wave Spots

Explore the best breaks around Paddarai — from mellow rollers to hollow barrels

Intermediate

Roxi's

Roxi's is one of the closest waves to Paddarai Surf Lodge, featuring consistent, long, powerful reef breaks that can get even bigger during the rainy season—perfect for beginners

Malakopa 3–12 ft
Advanced

Rags Left

Powerful hollow right breaking over shallow reef. Favoured by experienced surfers for its fast, barreling sections on larger swells.

Petojetsabu Island 3–15 ft
Beginner

Rags Right

Gentle, slow-breaking waves over a sandy bottom — the ideal spot to learn and build confidence in a beautiful, uncrowded setting.

Petojetsabu Island 3–10 ft
Advanced

Thunders

A remote A-frame peak accessed only by boat, breaking both left and right into deep water. Rarely surfed — a true hidden gem of the Mentawai chain.

Sibigau Island 3–12 ft
Intermediate

Rednuht

A classic right-point break that works best on the afternoon glass. Famous for its scenic backdrop and long, workable walls perfect for cutbacks.

Sibigau Island 4–12 ft
Intermediate

Greenbush

Crystal-clear water, turquoise reef, and mechanical lefts that peel for over 200 meters. One of the most photogenic and consistent breaks near the lodge.

Betumonga 2–6 ft
Advanced

Macaronis

One of the most iconic right-handers in the Mentawai archipelago. This world-class reef break delivers long, perfectly shaped tubes that challenge even the most seasoned surfers.

Silabu 5–12 ft
